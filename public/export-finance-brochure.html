The problem

Most exporters are unknowingly financing their buyers.

You raise the invoice. You bear the cost of waiting. Your buyer gets 30, 60, sometimes 120 days of free credit, funded entirely by your working capital.

That's capital you can't deploy. Stock you can't order. Growth you can't fund.

The Akro solution

We buy your invoice. You get paid. Your buyer settles later.

The moment your shipment is verified, we release up to 90% of the invoice value. Completely collateral-free. And because it's non-recourse, buyer default risk sits entirely with us, not you.

Built for Indian exporters. Backed by our global trade finance network spanning 70+ countries.
